Reward

All of your donations will be given a estimate price which will be decided by DonateKaro.

The value of all your donations will be sumed up.

When the total is above ₹1000 you are eligible for rewards.

  • If the total of estimated price of all the donations is in the range of ₹1000-₹4999 (inclusive) then you will receive 10% of the total as reward.

  • If the total of estimated price of all the donations is in the range of ₹5000-₹9999 (inclusive) then you will receive 15% of the total as reward.

* Transactions will be online (Through paytm).
